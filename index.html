Who are we

British Bakery is India's leading online cake portal, accepting orders through its website and delivering to over 1000 cities in India. It was started by Mr. Saiyam Jain in 2020 and is based in New Delhi. We specialize in birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, wedding cakes, cartoon cakes, fondant cakes, pastries and donutsâ€Œ.
